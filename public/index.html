Grid

Round time

    1–4 humans: bots fill the match to 6. With 5–6 humans, no bots.

    How to play

    1. Each blink telegraphs for a split second (dim ring) before you can tap — watch for it, then react.
    2. Tiles blink on the grid at random. Keep your eyes on the board! Host can pick 5×5 up to 16×16 — bigger boards run longer.
    3. Tap a blinking tile before it vanishes to claim it and score.
    4. Special tiles: ⚡ double, 💣 minus one, ❄️ freeze rivals, 🔥 streak combo, 🌀 shuffle owners, 🧲 magnet vacuum.
    5. Chain quick claims within half a second for combo bonus points. Empty seats can be filled by friendly bots.
    6. When time runs out, the highest score wins.

    Settings

    Tip: keep sound low for office play. Shape hints add stripes and borders so specials are easier to tell apart.

    About

    Blink Grid is a tiny real-time reaction game: shared grid, random blinks, first tap wins the tile. Built with Node.js and Socket.io — no database, just in-memory fun.
